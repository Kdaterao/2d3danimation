# What is premultiplication

* Premultiplication is the act of multiplying our alpha channels onto our RGB channels before processing them 


# Why/when to do premultiplication 

* Premultiplication (multiplying RGB color channels by the Alpha channel) should be used before merging, blending, or filtering images with transparency to prevent dark or light fringes. It is essential in VFX, compositing, and rendering to ensure that transparent areas properly integrate with background colors.
